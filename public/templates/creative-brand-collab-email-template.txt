CREATIVE BRAND COLLAB - Partnership Email Template
====================================================

Instructions: Fill in the [bracketed] sections with your info.

----------------------------------------------------

Subject: Partnership Idea for [Brand Name]

Hi [Name]!

Hope you're doing well! We connected on [Platform] and you mentioned I should email you about a collaboration idea.

First - [Brand Name] is genuinely one of my favorites. I wanted to pitch you before anyone else.

The Idea:
[Describe your unique concept - make it creative and specific to the brand]

The Proposal:
I'm hosting [event/campaign description] for [number] [audience type] and want to partner with 1-2 aligned brands like yours. Here's what you'd get:

• [Deliverable 1 - e.g., 2 Instagram Reels featuring your products]
• [Deliverable 2 - e.g., 5 Stories throughout the event]
• [Deliverable 3 - e.g., Products sent to all attendees with their own audiences]
• [Deliverable 4 - e.g., Cross-promotion from all participants]
• [Deliverable 5 - e.g., Association with your cause/mission]

I'll cover [your contribution] - you provide [their contribution]. [Brand Name] came to mind immediately because of your values and mission.

Interested in exploring this?

Excited to hear your thoughts,
[Your Name]
[Contact Info]

P.S. - Happy to jump on a quick call to walk through the full vision! 📞

----------------------------------------------------

TIPS FOR USING THIS TEMPLATE:
• Create a unique concept that can't be easily replicated - brands love exclusivity
• Quantify your reach and audience demographics
• Show you've done your homework on the brand's values
• Offer to cover logistics to make it easy for them to say yes
