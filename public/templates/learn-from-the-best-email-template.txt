LEARN FROM THE BEST - Mentorship Request Email Template
====================================================

Instructions: Fill in the [bracketed] sections with your info.

----------------------------------------------------

Subject: Fellow [School/Industry] Person - Quick Question

Hey [Name]!

I'm [Your Name], founder of [Your Company] - [one-sentence description]. I'm also a [School/Shared Background] alum!

Your [podcast/company/content] is AMAZING. I'm at the stage where I'm trying to figure out how to [your current challenge - e.g., "turn engaged listeners into an active community"], and you've clearly mastered this.

Would love 30 minutes to pick your brain about your growth strategy. Happy to buy you coffee (virtual or IRL) or pay for your time if that's easier.

Any chance you're free next week?

Appreciate you,
[Your Name]

P.S. - If timing sucks, totally get it. Just had to try! 💪

----------------------------------------------------

TIPS FOR USING THIS TEMPLATE:
• Find a genuine shared connection (school, industry, mutual interest)
• Be specific about what you want to learn - vague asks get ignored
• Offering to pay for their time shows you value their expertise
• Keep it casual and authentic - overly formal emails feel transactional
