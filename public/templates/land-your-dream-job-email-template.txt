LAND YOUR DREAM JOB - Cold Outreach Email Template
====================================================

Instructions: Fill in the [bracketed] sections with your info.

----------------------------------------------------

Subject: [Company Name] + My [Expertise Area]

Hi [Name],

I'm genuinely excited about what [Company Name] is building, and I have [insert your expertise OR why you personally connect with their mission].

Here's what I bring:

• [Top accomplishment with metric/link - one sentence max]
• [Second accomplishment with metric/link - one sentence max]

I'd love to learn about your biggest challenges right now and share how I can help solve them. I'm happy to volunteer my time first to prove my value to the team.

Are you free for a quick 15-minute call this week?

Thanks,
[Your Name]
[LinkedIn Profile]

P.S. - No pressure if timing isn't right. Just wanted to shoot my shot! 🚀

----------------------------------------------------

TIPS FOR USING THIS TEMPLATE:
• Research the company thoroughly before reaching out - mention specific projects or values
• Keep your accomplishments quantified (e.g., "increased revenue by 40%")
• Offering to volunteer shows confidence and removes risk for the hiring manager
• Follow up once after 5-7 days if you don't hear back
