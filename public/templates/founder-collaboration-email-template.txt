FOUNDER COLLABORATION REQUEST - Email Template
====================================================

Instructions: Fill in the [bracketed] sections with your info.

----------------------------------------------------

Subject: Quick Collab Idea from [Your Company]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], founder of [Your Company] - [one-sentence description of what you do].

I've heard amazing things about you from [mutual connection/team name] and I'm a huge fan of your [specific thing they do/philosophy/work]. Would love <20 minutes to tell you more about what we're building and explore potential collaboration.

Looking forward to connecting,
[Your Name]
[LinkedIn/Website]

P.S. - Just listened to [their podcast/read their article] - your take on [specific point] was 🔥

----------------------------------------------------

TIPS FOR USING THIS TEMPLATE:
• Always mention a mutual connection or shared community if possible
• Reference something specific about their work to show genuine interest
• Keep the ask small (under 20 minutes) to increase response rates
• The P.S. adds a personal touch - make it authentic
