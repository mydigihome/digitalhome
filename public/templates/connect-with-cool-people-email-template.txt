CONNECT WITH COOL PEOPLE - Networking Email Template
====================================================

Instructions: Fill in the [bracketed] sections with your info.

----------------------------------------------------

Subject: Love What You're Building

Hi [Name],

I'm really impressed by what [Company] is building, and I'm [your current role/goal - e.g., "growing my podcast community" or "building in the creator space"].

A bit about me:

• [Top accomplishment with numbers/links - one sentence]
• [Second accomplishment with numbers/links - one sentence]

I'd love to learn more about [specific aspect of their journey - e.g., "how you scaled to 100K users" or "your approach to community building"]. No pitch, just genuinely want to connect with people doing cool work in this space.

Free for 15 minutes this week?

Best,
[Your Name]
[LinkedIn Profile]

P.S. - Zero agenda here - just admire what you're building! ✨

----------------------------------------------------

TIPS FOR USING THIS TEMPLATE:
• Lead with genuine admiration - people can tell when you're being authentic
• Share your accomplishments briefly so they know you're serious
• "No pitch" and "zero agenda" removes pressure and increases replies
• Follow up with value (share an article, intro, etc.) after the call
